Why everyone should skateboard

  1. Skateboarding is fun
    • It's a great way to make friends and socialize
    • There's nothing quite as satisfying as learning a new skateboard trick
  2. It's good for your health
    • It's great cardio exercise, good for your lungs and heart.
    • It's good for your mental health, it teaches resiliance in the face of difficulty.
